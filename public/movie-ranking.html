Review of the Best Movies According to Me

My top 4 favourite movies.

1. Jagun Jagun

It is a captivating Nollywood epic blending action and tradition, with powerful performances.

2. Avatar: The Last Airbender

It is beautifully crafted animated series with deep character development, and timeless themes of balance and hope. I really love the role of Aang in the movie.

3. Coco

It s a heartwarming anime of family, culture, and the power of memory, with a soundtrack that sticks.

4. Artemis Fowl

It is a movie that captures the magic of the books and courage of a teen to save his father.

5. Avatar: The Way of Water

It is a anime and a sci-fi that illustrated a survival of a family in the oceans.
